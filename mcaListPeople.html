References:


People: a filterable list of all people in the ancestry
Profile: a profile of an individual person including source references
Marriages: a filterable list of marriages (where the marriage date or marriage place are known)
Residents: a list of people and places where they lived, for primary addresses over extended periods
References: a list of references for people in the ancestry, tied to each person's ID
Cohorts: a list of people with known birth dates and their assocaiated cohort (e.g. baby boomer)
Birthdays: a list of upcoming birthdays showing the number of months away from each birthday
