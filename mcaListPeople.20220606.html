People Profile Unions Residents References Cohorts B'days Options

To find a person's profile, find their Person ID then click 'Profile', enter the Person ID and click 'Submit'.
To find a Person ID, click 'People' then 'Submit' and scan the list. Filter the list by entering text in the 'Filter'.
Every person's profile has their ancestry in a tree layout at the bottom of the person's profile results.
The 'Results' box can be expanded by dragging the bottom right corner with a mouse.
